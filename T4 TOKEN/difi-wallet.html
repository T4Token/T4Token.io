T4 Defi Wallet

Your crypto funds are safe with the T4 DeFi wallet. T4 Token generates a non-custodial wallet for its users. The private keys in our non-custodial wallets are held by the wallet owners, who have total control over their currencies and tokens. Wallet holders are solely liable for the protection and confidentiality of their assets. While using the DeFi wallet, you can link it to any cryptocurrency exchange or dApp or for trading activities and then disconnect it. T4 Token's non-custodial wallets are a superior solution for real-time blockchain development solutions.

T4 Token offers an array of DeFi utilities in one place through its non-custodial wallet. Here are some stirring features of T4 DeFi Wallet:

SWAP

Swapping is made simple thanks to the built-in capabilities of T4 DeFi wallets, which allow users to easily link their wallets on other platforms and conduct token swaps with their clients with little friction. Our DeFi wallet supports multi-chain swapping of 300+ tokens. Some examples are BSC Chain, Ethereum, Polygon Networks, and Solana. It uses the in-app bridging function to transfer assets between other chains and Cronos.

Deposit NFTs

The non-fungible tokens may be deposited, viewed, shared, and the characteristics of their rarity studied with ease. Numerous NFT forms on multiple blockchains are supported.

Store Any Cryptocurrency

Send and receive any cryptocurrency or token. You may keep any cryptocurrency in storage, and you'll have total control over your private keys. The BSC Chain, Polygon Networks, and Ethereum blockchains are just a few of the more than 15 supported where you can easily manage 700+ tokens. You can also conveniently import your current wallet with a 12/18/24-word recovery phrase.
